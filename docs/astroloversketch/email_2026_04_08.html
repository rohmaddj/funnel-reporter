712 checkout clicks generated only 15 sales (2.11%) — a catastrophic 88% drop below minimum viability. This single issue is costing $4,900+ weekly in lost FE revenue alone. v2 at 5.65% proves traffic quality is adequate; v4's checkout page or ClickBank integration is broken.
