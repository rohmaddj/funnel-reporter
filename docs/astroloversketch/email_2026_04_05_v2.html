CRITICAL #1 — ClickBank checkout conversion (v4) observed

7.39% checkout-to-purchase is less than half the 15-20% minimum target. 1,341 people abandoned payment out of 1,448 checkout clicks — this represents 92.6% cart abandonment on ClickBank's page.

  • Audit ClickBank checkout page: test different payment gateway configurations, ensure mobile optimization, verify no SSL/trust badge issues
  • A/B test checkout button copy on offer page: 'Secure Your Reading' vs 'Get Instant Access' vs 'Complete Order' to pre-qualify buyer intent
  • Add exit-intent popup on offer page with urgency/scarcity before checkout click to increase purchase commitment
  • Review offer page final CTA section: add trust seals, money-back guarantee prominence, testimonials immediately above checkout button
  • Implement ClickBank's 'QuickPay' option if not active — reduces checkout friction for returning ClickBank customers
Revenue impact: Improving from 7.4% to 18% (conservative target) = +154 weekly sales = +$5,698 weekly frontend revenue (+83% lift). At current $58.57 AOV, total revenue would reach $15,900/week.
HIGH #2 — OTO2 upsell sequence optimization observed

OTO2 (Timeline $47) converts at only 12.8% with minimal downsell uptake (1.7%). Only 17 total OTO2 sales from 117 buyers suggests a broken redirect, poor offer match, or timing issue after OTO1.

  • Verify ClickBank OTO2 redirect is firing correctly after OTO1 accept/decline — check for broken pixels or URL parameters
  • Test OTO2 offer immediately after frontend purchase (before OTO1) for a segment — Timeline may have broader appeal than Blueprint
  • Rewrite OTO2 sales page: emphasize 'complete your astrology toolkit' angle and show how Timeline complements the sketch (not Blueprint)
  • Increase OTO2 downsell discount to $27 (from $37) to create stronger value perception vs main $47 offer
  • A/B test video VSL for OTO2 vs current text sales letter — backend offers often perform better with personality-driven video
Revenue impact: Improving OTO2 combined rate from 14.5% to 25% (strong benchmark) = +12 weekly sales = +$564-705 weekly backend revenue. At scale, this adds $2,300-2,900 monthly.
